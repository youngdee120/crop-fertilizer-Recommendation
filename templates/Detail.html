Fertilizer Recommendation System

The Fertilizer Recommendation System is a smart agricultural tool designed to help farmers make informed decisions about the types and quantities of fertilizers to use on their crops. By analyzing soil data, crop types, and local weather conditions, this system provides personalized fertilizer recommendations to optimize crop yield and reduce input costs.

Key points used in projects are mentioned below:

N (Nitrogen)

Nitrogen is crucial for plant growth, food processing, and chlorophyll creation.

P (Phosphorus)

Phosphorus helps store and transfer energy in plants for growth and reproduction.

K (Potassium)

Potassium is essential for plant development and overall health.

Temperature

High temperatures impact photosynthesis and respiration in plants.

Humidity

High humidity can cause mold, bacterial growth, and invite pests.

Moisture

Soil moisture affects nutrient availability and crop health.

Soil Type

Different soil types impact crop growth—loamy soil is often ideal.

Label

The system identifies 22 different fertilizer types based on analysis.
